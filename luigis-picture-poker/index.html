How to Play Luigi's Picture Poker

1. Start with 10 coins

2. Click "Deal Cards" to begin a round

3. Select cards you want to discard by clicking them

4. Click "Discard Selected" to replace your chosen cards

5. Click "Continue" to reveal the winner

6. Win coins by getting better hands than Luigi!
